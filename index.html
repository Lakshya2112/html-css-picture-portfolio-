GET TO KNOW ME.....

Hello everyone, my name is Lakshya Malhotra. I'm a student and also a self taught photography enthusiast from the Pink city of Jaipur, Rajasthan. Currently I am pursuing my undergrad in Computer Science and Engineering from VIT Vellore. I started clicking pictures since I was in 9th grade. I worked on my skills while attending high school and slowly inclined towards learning professional photography. My favourite areas of interest in photography include Astrophotography, Travel Photography and Street Photography. Since high school, I have captured many pictures. Some of my favourite shots are displayed here.
